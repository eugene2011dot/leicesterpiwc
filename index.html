About Us

Welcome to Leicester Pentecost International Worship Centre (PIWC), a vibrant and diverse church community in Leicester. As part of the Church of Pentecost, we are dedicated to fostering spiritual growth and community through worship, discipleship, and service.

Our mission is to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ and create a welcoming environment where individuals and families can grow in faith. Whether you are a lifelong Christian or exploring your faith for the first time, we are committed to providing an inclusive and supportive atmosphere for all.

At Leicester PIWC, we believe in the power of worship and prayer to transform lives. Our Sunday services are filled with dynamic praise, inspirational teachings, and a strong sense of fellowship. In addition to our worship services, we host a variety of Bible studies, youth programmes, and community outreach initiatives, aimed at building both spiritual and practical connections within the local and global community.

Join us as we pursue a life of purpose, faith, and love, rooted in the teachings of Christ. We look forward to welcoming you into our church family.
