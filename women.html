Women Ministry

'Holiness! Unto the LORD!'

- Women's Ministry Slogan

Who We Are

At Pentecost International Worship Centre (PIWC), our vibrant Women Ministry is dedicated to the spiritual, social, and holistic growth of women aged 18 and above. We believe in empowering women to live out their faith and make a positive impact in their communities. The ministry nurture and equip women to grow in their relationship with God, develop their unique gifts, and be unleashed into their spheres of influence to share God’s love.

Our Vision

To see women transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit, living lives of holiness and making a difference in their families, workplaces, and communities.

What We Do

The Media Team plays a critical role in various aspects of church life, including:

  • Spiritual Growth: Bible studies, prayer meetings, and spiritual retreats to deepen women’s faith and understanding of God’s Word.
  • Social Development: Workshops and seminars on various topics such as health, finance, marriage, and personal development to enhance women’s overall well-being.
  • Holistic Growth: Programs that address the physical, emotional, and mental health of women, promoting a balanced and healthy lifestyle.
  • Community Outreach: Organizing outreach initiatives to support and uplift the local community, demonstrating God’s love in practical ways.

Get Involved

Are you tech-savvy or interested in learning more about media production? The Media Team is always looking for new members who are willing to contribute their skills or grow in this exciting ministry. Whether you’re experienced or just getting started, there’s a place for you to serve.

Join Us

If you would like to join the Media Team, please reach out to us at media@leicesterpiwc.org or speak to one of our team members after service. We’d love to have you on board as we continue to use media to glorify God and connect with our community.
