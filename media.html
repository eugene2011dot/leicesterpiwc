Media Team

Who We Are

The Media Team at Leicester PIWC is committed to enhancing the worship experience through technology and creative communication. We are a group of passionate individuals who work behind the scenes to ensure that every service, event, and special occasion is broadcasted, recorded, and shared with excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to use digital platforms and media technology to spread the gospel, keeping our church connected both within and beyond the walls of the physical building. By leveraging modern tools, we aim to create an immersive worship experience, reaching souls far and wide.

What We Do

The Media Team plays a critical role in various aspects of church life, including:

  • Live Streaming: Broadcasting Sunday services and special events on YouTube, Facebook, and other social media platforms to reach those who cannot attend in person.
  • Audio-Visual Setup: Managing the sound, lighting, and video systems to ensure a smooth, distraction-free worship experience.
  • Photography & Videography: Capturing high-quality images and videos during services, events, and outreach activities for the church’s records and online sharing.
  • Social Media Management: Managing our online presence by creating and sharing engaging content that reflects our church’s mission and values.
  • Graphic Design & Visual Content Creation: Designing flyers, posters, and other promotional material for church programs and initiatives.
  • Website Management: Ensuring the church’s website is up-to-date, user-friendly, and serves as a valuable resource for members and visitors alike.

Get Involved

Are you tech-savvy or interested in learning more about media production? The Media Team is always looking for new members who are willing to contribute their skills or grow in this exciting ministry. Whether you’re experienced or just getting started, there’s a place for you to serve.

Join Us

If you would like to join the Media Team, please reach out to us at media@leicesterpiwc.org or speak to one of our team members after service. We’d love to have you on board as we continue to use media to glorify God and connect with our community.
