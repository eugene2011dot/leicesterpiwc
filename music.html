Music Team

Who We Are

The Music Team at Leicester PIWC is a vibrant group of musicians and vocalists committed to creating a powerful worship atmosphere. We believe in the transformative power of music and its ability to connect hearts to God during worship services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead worship that inspires, uplifts, and draws people closer to God. We strive to use our musical gifts to express our faith and encourage the congregation in their spiritual journey, fostering a sense of community through shared musical experiences.

What We Do

The Music Team engages in various activities to enrich the church's worship experience, including:

  • Worship Leading: Guiding the congregation in worship during services through dynamic music and songs.
  • Instrumental Performance: Providing musical accompaniment with various instruments during worship and events.
  • Vocal Ensembles: Creating harmony and depth through choir and vocal group performances.
  • Song Selection: Curating songs that align with the churchâ€™s vision and theme for each service.
  • Rehearsals: Regular practice sessions to prepare for Sunday services and special events.
  • Community Events: Participating in outreach programs and community gatherings to share the message of God through music.

Get Involved

Whether you are a seasoned musician or just starting, the Music Team welcomes anyone with a passion for worship through music. Join us in creating an uplifting environment where your talents can flourish and contribute to our worship services.

Join Us

If you would like to join the Music Team, please reach out to us at music@leicesterpiwc.org or connect with one of our members after service. We look forward to welcoming you to our family as we glorify God through music.
