Probationary Overseer Michael Frimpong is the minister in charge of Leicester PIWC. He joined the church on 25th August 2024, transitioning from James McKeown PIWC. His leadership brings a wealth of spiritual insight and pastoral care to the congregation.

Ministerial Journey

With a deep passion for the Word of God and a commitment to shepherding the flock, Probationary Overseer Frimpong has served in various capacities within The Church of Pentecost. His transition to Leicester PIWC marks a new chapter in his ministry, where he continues to inspire and guide believers in their faith journey.

Vision and Mission

His mission is to nurture spiritual growth, encourage unity, and ensure the church remains rooted in sound doctrine. Under his leadership, Leicester PIWC aims to strengthen its evangelism efforts and provide a supportive environment for all members.
