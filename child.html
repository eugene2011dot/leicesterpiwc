Children's Team

Who We Are

The Children's Team at Leicester PIWC is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth and development of children through creative teaching, fun activities, and engaging worship. We are passionate about creating a safe and joyful environment where children can learn about God and develop a strong foundation in their faith.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead children to a personal relationship with Jesus Christ by teaching them biblical principles and helping them build a lifelong faith. We aim to provide spiritual guidance while encouraging creativity, curiosity, and community among the children we serve.

What We Do

The Children’s Team is involved in a variety of activities to support the children of the church, including:

  • Sunday School: Weekly Bible lessons designed to help children understand the Word of God in an age-appropriate way.
  • Children's Worship: Leading dynamic and interactive worship experiences that engage children in praise and worship.
  • Vacation Bible School: Organising special holiday programmes filled with fun, learning, and spiritual growth.
  • Community Outreach: Hosting events and activities that involve children in the wider community, promoting kindness, compassion, and service.
  • Creative Arts: Encouraging children to express their faith through music, drama, and arts and crafts.

Get Involved

If you have a heart for children and a desire to see them grow in their faith, we welcome you to join our team! Whether you have experience in teaching, creative arts, or simply love working with kids, there are many ways you can get involved.

Join Us

If you’d like to become part of the Children’s Team, please reach out to us at children@leicesterpiwc.org or speak to one of our team members after service. We’re excited to have you help shape the future of our children’s ministry!
