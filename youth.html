Youth Ministry

WHO ARE WE?

CHURCH OF PENTECOST LEICESTER YOUTH & PENSA MINISTRY

VISION

To wholeheartedly transform young people towards possessing unyielding faith and stimulating an appetite for missions so as to reconstruct the parameters of thought, values, practices, social patterns, relationships, and decision-making in every sphere of society.

LANGUAGE

To habituate and equip our young people in doctrine so as to enable them to be fluent in the language of the kingdom.

SOCIAL INFLUENCE

Dispatch our youth as social influencing channels through which faith in God, hope from the gospel, and the matchless love of God will emanate the nation’s youth culture, reach the lost, and transform society.

COMMUNITY

Strengthen relationships, build a community, and engage missions as an extension of the community. The objective is to possess the nation’s youth as a community.
