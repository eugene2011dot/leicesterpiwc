Services

Have a look at our weekly activities

SUNDAY

Sunday Service

Sunday Service takes place every Sunday from 10:00 AM to 12:30 PM at Leicester PIWC. It includes spirit-filled worship, Bible-based preaching, prayer, and fellowship. There are also children and youth services to engage the younger ones. All are welcome!

MONDAY

Youth Ministry Online Meetings

Our Youth Ministry Online Meetings take place every Monday at 7:00 PM on Microsoft Teams. These sessions include Bible studies, discussions on faith and personal growth, and interactive Q&A sessions. It's a great time for young people to connect, learn, and grow spiritually together.

TUESDAY

Dawn Prayers

Tuesday Dawn Prayers take place every Tuesday at 6:00 AM on Microsoft Teams. It’s a time of early morning worship, intercession, and seeking God’s guidance to start the day with spiritual strength.

THURSDAY

Prayer Clinic

Thursday Prayer Clinic is held every Thursday at 11:00 AM on Microsoft Teams. It is a dedicated time for intense prayer, healing, and spiritual breakthroughs, where members gather to seek God's presence and power.

FRIDAY

Transformation Fridays

Transformation Fridays take place every Friday at 7:30 PM. It is a powerful time of worship, teaching, and personal transformation, equipping believers to grow in faith and impact their world.
