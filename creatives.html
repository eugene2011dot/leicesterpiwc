Creative Arts Team

Who We Are

The Creative Arts Team at Leicester PIWC is a collective of talented individuals who use their gifts to enhance the worship experience through various forms of art. From music and dance to drama and visual arts, we are passionate about glorifying God with our creativity and engaging the congregation in meaningful worship.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and uplift the church through creative expressions of faith. We believe that art is a powerful tool to communicate the gospel and enhance worship. By using our talents, we aim to create an atmosphere where people can connect with God on a deeper level.

What We Do

The Creative Arts Team contributes to the church in various ways, including:

  • Music Ministry: Leading worship through music, ensuring that every service is filled with heartfelt praise and adoration.
  • Dance and Drama: Using expressive movements and acting to tell stories of faith, hope, and love during services and special events.
  • Visual Arts: Creating stunning visual representations of biblical themes through painting, drawing, and digital art.
  • Stage Design: Designing and setting up stage backgrounds and props to visually enhance worship services and events.
  • Special Performances: Organising performances for Christmas, Easter, and other church celebrations to bring the message of Christ to life in creative ways.

Get Involved

If you’re passionate about using your creative talents for the glory of God, we’d love to have you join the Creative Arts Team. Whether you’re a musician, dancer, actor, or visual artist, there’s a place for you to contribute your gifts to the church.

Join Us

If you’re interested in joining the Creative Arts Team, please contact us at creativearts@leicesterpiwc.org or speak to a team member after the service. Let’s use our creativity to inspire worship and glorify God together!
