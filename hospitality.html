Hospitality Team

Who We Are

The Hospitality Team at Leicester PIWC is dedicated to making every member and visitor feel welcomed and valued. We serve as the church's warm and inviting face, ensuring that everyone who walks through our doors experiences the love of Christ through our service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an atmosphere of kindness, warmth, and inclusivity. We aim to embody the spirit of Christian hospitality, where every guest is treated like family, and every need is met with grace and attentiveness.

What We Do

The Hospitality Team plays a crucial role in ensuring that services and events run smoothly, including:

  • Welcoming Guests: Greeting every person who arrives for service with a smile, providing assistance as needed, and ensuring a welcoming environment.
  • Visitor Follow-up: Connecting with new visitors, offering information about the church, and maintaining a positive relationship to encourage their return.
  • Event Support: Helping to plan, set up, and serve during church events such as community meals, conferences, and special services.
  • Refreshment Preparation: Organising and serving refreshments during or after services, making sure that everyone feels cared for.
  • Ushering: Assisting with seating arrangements, distributing materials such as bulletins or offering envelopes, and maintaining the order of services.
  • Care Ministry: Reaching out to those in need within the church, offering support through meals, visits, or prayers during difficult times.
